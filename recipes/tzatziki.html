Traditional Greek Tzatziki

I recently came back from Crete Greece and whilst I was out there I found a new food I had spent 30 years of my life not knowing, Tzatziki For those of you that haven't lived under a rock like myself I'm sure you are all very aware of this Greek dish already. On the very slim chance you have lived under a rock let me explain it.

Tzatziki is a simple Greek dip. It's super easy to make and can go with pretty much anything (I should know I tried alotttttt of other foods with my new found love)
