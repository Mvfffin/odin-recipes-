Everybody Loves SpagBol!

This recipe is easy to follow, delicious and cheap to make! Why would you not want to follow along? In this easy to follow recipe we will be creating the classic Italian dish. So grab your ingredients, sort your playlist and lets get cooking!

With minced meat, just a few herbs, our special sauce and of course spaghetti, you can create a classic Italian dish

Okay so how are we going to be creating this classicdish and what ingredients will we need. You will find all the answers to this below

Ingredients

  • Any minced Pork and Beef 50/50 of each
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Spaghetti
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Basil
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cheese!(we all love cheese)

Steps

  1. Start by getting the oil hot in the frying pan
  2. Boil some water
  3. Add salt to water and place spaghetti inside until and let cook until 'Al dente'
  4. Throw in the already chopped vegetables
  5. Take vegetables out of the frying pan and lay to one side
  6. Throw in your meat and cook until brown
  7. Add vegetables back to the pan with the meat inside
  8. Add the chopped tomatoes
  9. Add all your seasonings
  10. Let simmer for 20-30 minutes
  11. Grate your selected cheese (my personal favourite to put on this dish is parmesan)
  12. Plate up and enjoy!
